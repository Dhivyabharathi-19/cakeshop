Holy Cake means 'Food of the Gods', a name truly befitting our exclusive and indulgent
range of offerings, including brownies, cakes, desserts, chocolates, breads, and
savouries.

From our humble beginnings in Tamil Nadu, when the first Holy Cake patisserie opened
its doors in 2004 at a cozy corner in Chennai, we’ve grown to
become a cherished name across the state.

Our mission is to spread happiness by serving smiles on a plate — and this journey
continues as we expand Holy Cake patisseries across Tamil Nadu,
sharing moments of sweetness with every bite.
